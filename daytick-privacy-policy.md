# Privacy Policy - DayTick

**Last Updated:** November 2025

DayTick is an offline task and note management application. This application does not require an internet connection and does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Information Collection and Use
DayTick does **not** collect any personal information from users.  
All tasks, notes, and reminder data is stored **locally on the user's device**.  
We do **not** use any external servers, databases, analytics, or cloud services.

## Permissions
DayTick may request the following basic permissions:

- **Notification Access** – Used only to display reminders and alerts.
- **Local Storage Access** – Used only to save tasks and notes on your device.

We **do not** use tracking technologies, analytics tools, or identifiers.

## Third-Party Sharing
DayTick **does not share any data** with any third-party services or individuals.

## Children’s Privacy
This app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any user data.

## Data Security
Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for managing your own backups.
